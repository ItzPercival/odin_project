Lasagna


Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta.
Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made
with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 400ºF. Arrange a rack in the middle of the oven and heat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Brown the beef and onion.
  3. Prepare the baking dish and assemble the meat sauce.
  4. Begin layering the lasagna.
  5. Continue layering the lasagna.
  6. Bake the lasagna for 1 hour.
